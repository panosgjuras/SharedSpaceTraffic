vehicle_dataset_shared_space.csv

vid: vehicle id
date: date of measurements
time: exact time vehicle detected
shared: if 1, shared space (i.e., Section B)
type: vehicle type
speed: vehicle speed in km/h
ped: pedestrian volume in peds/2 minutes
cross: average number of crossing in cross/2 minutes
headway: headway between two consecutive vehicle in seconds

pedestrian_dataset_shared_space.csv

pid: time interval id
date: date of measuarements
time: exact time of measurement (end of the 2 minutes - time interval)
shared: if 1, shared space (i.e., Section B)
ped: pedestrian volume in peds/2 minutes
cross: number of crossing in cross/2 minutes
headway: mean headway between two consecutive vehicle in seconds
